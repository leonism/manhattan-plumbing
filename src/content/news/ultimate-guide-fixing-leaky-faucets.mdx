## Common Types of Faucet Leaks

Before starting any repair, identify your faucet type:

1. **Compression Faucets**

   - Two handles for hot and cold
   - Uses rubber washers
   - Most common in older homes

2. **Ball-Type Faucets**

   - Single handle design
   - Contains a ball bearing
   - Common in kitchen sinks

3. **Cartridge Faucets**
   - Can be single or double handle
   - Uses cartridge for water control
   - Modern and reliable design

## Tools You'll Need

Gather these tools before starting:

- Adjustable wrench
- Phillips and flathead screwdrivers
- Pliers
- Replacement parts (washers, O-rings, cartridge)
- Plumber's grease
- Clean rags
